Frequently Asked Questions

Kazakhstan is rich in a variety of attractions, including natural and historical sites. Among them: Lake Balkhash, Charyn Canyon, Bayanaul National Park, the mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yassawi in Turkestan, and many others.

Kazakhstan has several national parks and reserves such as Bayanaul National Park, Kolsai Lakes National Park, Katon-Karagai National Park and Aksu-Zhabagly Reserve.

Visa requirements depend on the nationality of the visitor. Citizens of many countries can visit Kazakhstan without a visa for up to 30 days. It is recommended to check the current information on the official website of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Kazakhstan.

The official languages of Kazakhstan are Kazakh and Russian.

The currency of Kazakhstan is the Kazakhstani tenge (KZT).
